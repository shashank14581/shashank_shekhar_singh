FAQ What the work is really about
What are you building?

Tools and systems for agentic AI: agents that can reason through tasks, use tools, manage memory, and operate inside practical business workflows.

Why memory?

Because useful agents need more than long context. They need a way to decide what matters, what fades, what returns, and what becomes part of identity.

What is your edge?

I combine delivery experience, data science, software building, and first-principles thinking about how intelligent systems should behave.

Where should I start?

Start with agentic-ai-kit for implementation, then read the identity-conditioned forgetting paper to understand the memory thesis behind the work.
