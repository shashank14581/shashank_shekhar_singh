04 · Notes

What the work is really about

What are you building?

Tools and systems for agentic AI: agents that reason through tasks, use tools, manage memory, and operate inside real business workflows.

Why memory, specifically?

Because a useful agent needs more than a long context window. It needs a way to decide what matters, what fades, what returns, and what becomes part of its identity.

What’s your edge?

Delivery experience, applied data science, software building, and first-principles thinking about how intelligent systems should actually behave.

Where should I start?

Start with agentic-ai-kit for implementation, then read the identity-conditioned forgetting preprint to see the memory thesis behind it.
